Problem Statement

Traditional cropping patterns are not effective in today's rapidly changing, unpredicted climate and polluted soils. Likewise, every region is unique in relation to each other and has various properties. Our goal is to design a system to predict the most suitable crop for that region, and predict its yield

Keeping this in mind, we have created a web application which will help farmers to identify the best crop that they can grow in their respective fields, and predict its yield.
